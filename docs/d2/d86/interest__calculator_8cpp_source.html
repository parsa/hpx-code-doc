7 // Purpose: Calculates compound interest while using dataflow objects.
8 //
9 // In this example, you supply the program with the principal [$], the interest
10 // rate [%], the length of the compound period [months], and the length of time
11 // the money is invested [months]. The program will calculate the new total
12 // amount of money you have and the amount of interest made. For example if
13 // you have $100, an interest rate of 5%, a compound period of 6 months and
14 // you leave your money in that account for 36 months you will end up with
15 // $134.01 and will have made $34.01 in interest.
